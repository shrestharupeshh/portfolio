# Marathon
This portfolio has a section for my marathon.
It starts with me signing up for my first marathon. I signed up for my first marathon, the SF marathon, happening on July 26, 2026. I signed up on 6th of April and I had 16 weeks to train. So, I used Claude AI and ChatGPT to come up with my marathon plan. I used the best features of both, based on my health status and current running capacity/form. 

## Training Log
Claude then made me a great 16 week training plan for my marathon. It also built me a training log where I will feed the daily training data to Claude and it will add it to the html code. 

## Dashboard
It also built me a Dashboard which will track the logs entered and incorporate that in the dashboard and will show the overview of my training, with trends and estimated time of finish.
